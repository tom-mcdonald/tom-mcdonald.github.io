Tom McDonald

    Hello, I'm Tom

    I am a media anthropologist dedicated to using ethnographic engagement to achieve a richer understanding of how digital technologies, media and material culture come to mediate ongoing transformations in the communicative practices, economic behaviours, social relationships and human subjectivities of people in China and beyond. I joined the Department of Sociology at the University of Hong Kong in August 2015. Prior to this I was a Research Associate at the Department of Anthropology, University College London.
